## Looking for Real Bloggers

I'm sick of it. Absolutely sick of scrolling through endless AI-generated articles that all sound the same. Every blog post optimized for SEO, every title written by ChatGPT, every "helpful" content that reads like a robot trying to pretend it's human.

Remember when blogs were actually written by humans? When posts were rambling, messy, and full of personality? When people wrote about their Ben Nevis climbs or their childhood toys without worrying about keyword density?

## What I'm Looking For

I want to link to **real bloggers** who write:

- **Unfiltered opinions** that might offend someone
- **Personal stories** that actually happened
- **Rants about things** that piss them off
- **Weird hobbies** nobody cares about except them
- **Local content** that's too specific for a global audience
- **Writing with personality** instead of perfect grammar
- **Posts that take 3 hours to read** because they're rambling and wonderful

## What I'm NOT Looking For

- AI-generated "helpful" content
- Posts optimized for search engines instead of humans
- "10 Tips to Boost Your Productivity" (unless it's sarcastic)
- Anything that mentions "synergy" or "paradigm shift"
- Blogs with more ads than actual content
- Content farms churning out soulless articles

## Let's Exchange Links

If you run a real blog written by an actual human with thoughts and opinions and weird interests, I want to link to you.

**My requirements are simple:**
1. You're a real person (or at least write like one)
2. Your blog has personality
3. You're not just publishing AI slop
4. You're willing to link back to my nonsense

**What you get:**
- A permanent link on this page
- My eternal gratitude
