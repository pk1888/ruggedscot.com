My name is Paul. I'm 42 years old, born and bred in the rugged landscapes of Scotland. I've got a wife and two wee girls who keep me grounded and remind me what's truly important in life. This journal started as a way to document ma travels and recipes, but it's become something more.

To be honest, I'm fed up with the world today. It feels like we're being squeezed from every angle. The elites in their high towers seem more interested in their own power than the well being of the common man. Whether it's the constant surveillance, the manipulation of our food supply, or the blatant cloudseeding messing with our natural weather patterns, it's hard to stay silent.

It also feels like "AI slop" is taking over the internet, and finding genuine, real people behind websites is becoming harder every day. I'm looking to find like minded people so we can maybe exchange links and keep in touch in this increasingly artificial world.

I believe in returning to the basics. Climbing the Munros, baking ma own bread, and keeping a watchful eye on the systems that try to control us.

## The Tech Stack

This site is built with React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. While many of ma websites are hosted on ma own VPS, this particular site is hosted on GitHub Pages as a static site. I chose this approach because I wanted full control over ma content using Markdown files, without the bloat of traditional CMS platforms.

> **Disclaimer:** This site frame is built entirely with AI. I didn't want to use WordPress because I wanted to use Markdown for my posts and have full, total control, something current tools don't easily allow. Since I have very little coding skills, I used these new AI tools to build the frame. But make no mistake, while the code is AI, the posts and the thoughts are all real. No AI slop here.
