# Magical Isle of Skye

Skye is perhaps the most iconic of Scotland's islands. From the jagged peaks of the Cuillins to the turquoise waters of the Fairy Pools, every corner is a photographer's dream.

## Highlights
*   **The Old Man of Storr**: A steep hike but worth it for the otherworldly rock formations.
*   **The Fairy Pools**: Crystal clear pools at the foot of the Black Cuillins.
*   **Portree**: The charming capital with its colourful harbour.
