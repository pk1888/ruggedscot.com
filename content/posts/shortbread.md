# Traditional Scottish Shortbread

Shortbread is a staple of Scottish hospitality. This recipe has been in my family for three generations. The secret is high-quality butter and a bit of patience.

## Ingredients
*   250g Salted Butter (softened)
*   125g Caster Sugar
*   375g Plain Flour

## Method
1. Cream the butter and sugar together until smooth.
2. Stir in the flour to get a smooth paste.
3. Turn onto a work surface and gently roll out until 1cm thick.
4. Cut into rounds or "fingers" and prick with a fork.
5. Bake at 170°C for 15-20 minutes until pale golden.
