# Welcome to My Little Hill on the Web

Alright folks! Welcome to my little hill on the web. Well… not exactly a hill, but you get the idea.

I’ve always liked the idea of having my own wee corner of the internet. Somewhere to throw my thoughts, stories and whatever else happens to be rattling around my head.

I’ve got ADHD, so ma brain tends to bounce between topics like a sheep on espresso. One day I might be writing about mountains, the next I might be moaning about the price of a roll and sausage.

This blog will probably end up being a mix of things:

- Stories and adventures from around Scotland  
- Hill walks and mountains  
- Random thoughts about life  
- Good grub (and probably too much of it)  
- The occasional proper moan about the state of things  

Nothing too serious. Just whatever happens to be on my mind at the time.

I'll also not be focusing on "proper" English. In Scotland we sometimes use different words, so if you don't understand me, well you'll just have to ask me!

If you enjoy Scottish humour, the outdoors, and the occasional rant, you might find something you like here.

Maybe you'll find something interesting.  
Maybe you won't.

Either way, I'm glad you stopped by.

See you in the next one.
