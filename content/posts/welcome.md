# Welcome to my little hill on the web!

Alright folks! Welcome to my little hill on the web. Well, not exactly a hill, but you get my drift!

I've always wanted a home on the web but I've never really known what I wanted to say. I've always struggled to find the right words to express my thoughts and feelings. I do have ADHD so I'm not sure if this makes me struggle. 

Lately I've been feeling really weird about life. Everything feels really off. Times feel strange, people seem strange.

The world feels like a total lie now. "Does anyone else feel this way?"

- Food feels like poison
- Life-saving medications are hidden from the public  
- Government says one thing but does another
- Our country is being overrun
- We're becoming second-class citizens

I'm not sure what to make of it all, but I'm going to try and make some sense of it all and moan about it here. Maybe you feel the same way?

Maybe the Agenda 2030 is going to happen. The digital prison is already starting to be built here in the UK

Uch, I'm sure I'll have a right wee moan on here about that in the future, because it really is busting my tits!

Anyways, if you read this and feel the same way, feel free to drop me a line. I'm always up for a good moan!.

I'll use this blog to moan about whatever's on my mind, but I'll also try to write about some interesting things I come across around Scotland. 

Maybe you'll find something interesting here. Maybe you won't. Either way, I'm glad you're here!
